## 👥 Target Audience

### Primary Segments

#### 1. First-Time Homebuyers (Age: 28-40)

- **Profile:** Young professionals, newly married couples
- **Needs:** Affordable luxury, modern amenities, connectivity
- **Pain Points:** Budget constraints, trust issues with builders
- **Solution:** EMI-friendly plans, transparent documentation, proven delivery

#### 2. Real Estate Investors (Age: 35-55)

- **Profile:** HNIs, business owners, NRIs
- **Needs:** High ROI, capital appreciation, rental yield
- **Pain Points:** Market volatility, delayed projects
- **Solution:** Strategic locations, timely delivery, resale support

#### 3. Upgraders (Age: 40-60)

- **Profile:** Families seeking larger homes, better lifestyle
- **Needs:** Premium features, community living, security
- **Pain Points:** Compromising on quality, location limitations
- **Solution:** Luxury amenities, prime locations, integrated townships

#### 4. Commercial Investors

- **Profile:** Business owners, corporate entities
- **Needs:** Office spaces, retail outlets, warehousing
- **Pain Points:** Infrastructure gaps, regulatory hurdles
- **Solution:** DMIC corridor advantages, ready-to-move options

**Monitoring Tools:**

- Google Alerts for brand mentions
- Social listening tools (Brand24, Mention)
- Review monitoring across platforms

**Response Protocol:**

- Negative reviews: Respond within 24 hours
- Complaints: Escalate to customer service team
- Resolution: Public response + private follow-up
- Follow-up: Request updated review after resolution

**Crisis Scenarios:**

1. **Project Delay Concerns**
   - Proactive communication with timeline updates
   - Compensation offers if applicable
   - Regular progress videos/photos

2. **Quality Issues**
   - Immediate site inspection
   - Transparent rectification process
   - Third-party quality audit if needed

   - Fact-based response with evidence
   - Engage legal team if defamatory
